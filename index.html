About Orbit

Orbit is a lightweight background utility that prevents your system from going idle by intelligently moving the mouse cursor.

It checks every 30 seconds to see if the user has moved the mouse. If so, it does nothing.

But if the mouse stays stationary for 3 minutes, Orbit gently nudges the cursor in a random direction to keep your session active.

This makes Orbit perfect for staying connected during long meetings, avoiding screen timeouts, or keeping your display awake while you work hands-free.
